Baking Soda Volcano

Materials: Vinegar, Baking Soda, Food Coloring

Steps:

  1. Place baking soda in a cup.
  2. Add food coloring.
  3. Pour vinegar and watch the eruption!

Magic Milk Experiment

Materials: Milk, Dish Soap, Food Coloring

Steps:

  1. Pour milk onto a plate.
  2. Add drops of food coloring.
  3. Dip a cotton swab in dish soap and touch the milk – watch the colors dance!

Floating Egg

Materials: Water, Salt, Egg

Steps:

  1. Fill a glass with water.
  2. Place an egg inside – it will sink.
  3. Gradually add salt and stir – watch the egg float!
